Screwdriver
2 oz. Vodka
Orange Juice
Garnish: No Garnish
Pour vodka into a glass with ice and fill it with orange juice.

Harvey Wallbanger
2 oz. Vodka
Orange Juice
Float of Galliano® Liqueur
Garnish: No Garnish
Pour vodka into a glass with ice and almost fill it with orange juice. Pour a float of Galliano on top.

Gin and Tonic
2 oz. Gin
Tonic Water
Garnish: Lime Wedge
Pour gin into a glass with ice. Fill it with tonic water.

Pina Colada
2 oz. Rum (Light)
Piña Colada Mix
Splash of Pineapple Juice
Garnish: Pineapple Slice
In a blender combine the rum, piña colada mix and pineapple juice with crushed ice. Blend well at high speed and pour it into a hurricane or collins glass.

White Russian
1 1/2 oz. Vodka
1 oz. Coffee Liqueur
Cream / Half & Half
Garnish: No Garnish
Pour vodka and coffee liqueur into a glass with ice and add the cream or milk.

Gimlet
3 1/2 oz. Gin
1/2 oz. Lime Juice (Rose's®)
Garnish: Lime Wedge
Pour vodka or gin and the lime juice into a shaker with ice. Stir to chill the contents. Strain into a chilled martini glass.

Lemon Drop
3 oz. Citrus Vodka
1 oz. Triple Sec
1/2 oz. Lemon Juice
1 tsp. Sugar
Garnish: Lemon Wedge or Lemon Twist
Rub the rim of a chilled martini glass with a lemon wedge and dip it into sugar. Pour all ingredients into a shaker with ice. Shake and strain into a chilled martini glass.

Bloody Mary
2 oz. Vodka
3 Drops of Worcestershire Sauce
3 Drops of Tabasco® Sauce
1 tsp. Horseradish Sauce (Optional)
Dash of Lime Juice
Bloody Mary Mix
Garnish: Celery and/or Lime Wedge
Pour vodka into a salted glass with ice. Add the Worcestershire sauce, Tabasco sauce and lime juice. Fill the glass with tomato juice or Bloody Mary mix.

Irish Car Bomb

1/2 oz. Irish Cream
Stout
Garnish: No Garnish

Mojito
1 1/2 oz. Rum (Light)
12 Mint Leaves
1/2 oz. Lime Juice
1/2 oz. Simple Syrup
Club Soda / Carbonated Water
Garnish: Mint Leaves or Lime Wheel
In a Collins glass, place mint leaves, simple syrup and lime juice. Muddle them well. Fill glass with ice. Add rum and fill it with club soda.
